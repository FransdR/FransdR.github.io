I am a research scientist at Wageningen University and Research and part of the strategic capacity preparation team at ASML. Prior to joining ASML I was a senior consultant at CQM. With the help of analytics, I aim to make processes more resilient and sustainable. For this, I design and implement decision analytics solutions that make use of optimization, machine learning and artificial intelligence algorithms.

I have obtained my PhD in Operations Research at Tilburg University in the Netherlands advised by Dick den Hertog and Dimitris Bertsimas from MIT. Prior to starting my PhD research I obtained a degree in Applicable Mathematics (MSc.) from the London School of Economics and a degree in Management Science and Operations Research (MSc.) from Tilburg University. My main research interests are centered around adaptive optimization under uncertainty, supply chain resilience and optimization problems that arise in real-world applications.

See also my full Curricilum Vitae.

Some years ago I gave an optimization course for the JADS Data Expert Program, for which I make the materials available for other teachers here.

NEWS

-- Franz Edelman Finals

At CQM we were honored to be a finalist for the 2024 Franz Edelman Award in Orlando, Florida with the Transivion team. We designed and implemented an analytic solution for the mobility problem affecting hundreds of thousand elderly and disabled citizens within the Valys program in the Netherlands. Using state-of-the-art algorithms, we improved customer satisfaction and saved 100.000km a day. You can view a short summary vido presented at the Franz Edelman Gala below. We also made a podcast for the INFORMS series, which can be listened to here: ASML

-- August 2023: Dinalog project on supply chain resilience!

Recently, we at Wageningen were awarded the Restretch project by Dinalog together with JADS and a consortium of industry partners including Vos Logistics, Hoogwegt, Nobian and Vion. During the next year, we are going to develop new analytical methods to assess and improve supply chain resilience with practical applications in industry.
